# Content Ideas for Curious Things

## Platform Strategy

### TikTok (@curiousthings2023)
**Best performing content types:**
- Thrift/sourcing trips (the hunt)
- Gallery wall styling tips
- Behind-the-scenes packing/shipping
- Print making process
- "How to style" educational content

### Instagram (@curious_things2023)
**Focus:**
- Styled product photos
- Room inspiration
- Story highlights for categories
- Reels of TikTok content

### Facebook (Curious-Things2023)
**Focus:**
- Cross-post from Instagram
- Marketplace listings
- Community engagement

## Content Pillars

### 1. Sourcing/Thrifting
**Ideas:**
- "Come thrifting with me" vlogs
- "What I found today" hauls
- Estate sale previews
- Frame restoration before/after

### 2. Education/Styling
**Ideas:**
- "How to arrange a gallery wall"
- "Mixing frame styles"
- "Dark Academia room tour"
- "3 ways to style vintage frames"

### 3. Behind-the-Scenes
**Ideas:**
- Packing orders
- Printing process (Epson SC-P900)
- Organizing inventory
- Setting up for photos

### 4. Product Showcases
**Ideas:**
- "This week's favorite finds"
- "Just listed" announcements
- "Customer feature" (with permission)
- "From my shop to your home"
