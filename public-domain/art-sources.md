# Public Domain Art Sources

## Top Resources

### 1. Biodiversity Heritage Library (BHL)
- **URL:** biodiversitylibrary.org
- **Size:** 150,000+ illustrations
- **Best for:** Botanicals, insects, natural history
- **Download:** Free, high-res, no restrictions
- **Trending fits:** Dark Cottagecore, Nature Reclaims, Victorian botanicals

### 2. Internet Archive

#### Key Collections:

**"Illustrations of Exotic Entomology" (Drury, 1770s)**
- 650+ insect illustrations
- Exotic species, detailed scientific art
- Perfect for: Dark Cottagecore, Gothic natural history

**"British Entomology" (Curtis, 1823)**
- Insects on plants
- Shows interaction between flora and fauna
- Perfect for: Nature Reclaims, overgrown aesthetic

**"Collectanea Botanica" (Lindley)**
- Rare exotic plants
- Perfect for: Unique botanical prints

### 3. Wikimedia Commons
- **URL:** commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Botanical_illustrations
- **Best for:** Organized browsing by artist/work
- **Download:** Free, various resolutions

### 4. Picture Box Blue
- **URL:** pictureboxblue.com/botanicals
- **Best for:** Curated vintage botanicals
- **Style:** Ready-to-print, decorative focus

## Download Tips

- Look for highest resolution available (300 DPI minimum for prints)
- Check file format (TIFF/PNG best for quality, JPG for smaller files)
- Verify public domain status (pre-1929 usually safe)
- Save with descriptive filenames for organization
